Singapore Package

  •   5 Days
  •   4
  • Singapores
Day 1: Arrival in Singapore

Meet and greet at the airport and transfer to your hotel Check-in and relax In the evening, head out for a Night Safari Tour, an open-air zoo experience that showcases nocturnal animals in their natural habitats.

Day 2: Singapore City Tour

After breakfast, embark on a half-day city tour to explore the best of Singapore's attractions including Merlion Park, Gardens by the Bay, Chinatown, and Little India. In the afternoon, visit the world-famous Sentosa Island and enjoy the various attractions such as Cable car, Madame Tussauds 4 in 1 and Wings of Time show.

Day 3: Singapore Zoo

After lunch, visit the Singapore Zoo, one of the world's best rainforest zoos that features over 2,800 animals from 300 species.

Day 4: Marina Bay Sands and Garden By The Bay

Visit the Garden by the bay (flower dome and supertree) and Marina Bay Sands SkyPark

Day 5: Departure from Singapore
After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport for your flight back home. Note: This itinerary can be customized as per your preferences and budget.

Morning at leisure Transfer to airport for departure
